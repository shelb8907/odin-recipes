Nutella Hotteok

Description

You can change the fillings to any sweet combo such as peanut butter and jelly or brown sugar and pecans.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16.3 ounce) can refrigerated biscuits
  • 1/2 cup nutella
  • 2 tablespoons neutral oil

Steps

  1. Flatten each biscuit into a 4 inch circle. Place 1 tablespoon filling in the center of each biscuit. Wrap dough around the filling into a ball and crimp the dough to seal it tightly. Flatten the filled ball of dough into a patty shape.
  2. Heat a non stick skillet over medium-low heat. Add 2 teaspoon of oil and, working with 2 to 3 at a time, place the filled dough; seam side down into the skillet. Place lid on skillet and cook until the bottom side is golden brown and toasted, 3 to 4 minutes. Flip the pastries and cook until golden on both sides and the dough along the outside is cooked, an additional 3 to 4 minutes.
  3. Repeat with the remaining filled pastries and oil.
